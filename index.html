A big thank you to our research advisors Prof. Reid Simmons, Prof. Zackory Erickson and Dr. Pragathi Praveena for their guidance in this research!

A big thank you to my Marketing I (70-381) instructor Dr. Flora Feng for her guidance in this project.

I’m very grateful to my research mentor Dr. Bingquan Shen for his guidance in this research.
